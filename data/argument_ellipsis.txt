我看到了一个男孩，他也看到了___
----
我看到了一个男孩，他也看到了男孩。
# Translation: He saw a boy; I also saw a boy.
# Alphabet Romanization: Wo kan dao le yige nanhai, ta ye kan dao le nanhai.
# Kuan Tang. "Nominal Ellipsis in Mandarin" 

张三吃了苹果，李四还是不敢吃___
----
张三吃了苹果，李四还是不敢苹果。
# Translation: Zhang San ate an apple, Li Si still doesn't dare to eat an apple.
# Alphabet Romanization: Zhang San chi le pingguo, Li Si hai shi bu gan chi pingguo.
# Kuan Tang. "Nominal Ellipsis in Mandarin" 

他修车的方式比我的___更好。
----
他修车的方式比我的修车方式更好。
# Translation: His way of repairing cars is better than my way of repairing cars.
# Alphabet Romanization: Ta xiuche de fangshi bi wo de xiuche fangshi geng hao.
# Tang, Sze-Wing. "A parametric approach to NP ellipsis in Mandarin and Cantonese." Journal of East Asian Linguistics 20 (2011): 107-115.

他不来的原因我知道，但你的___我不知道。
----
他不来的原因我知道，但你的原因我不知道。
# Translation: I know why he didn't come, but I don't know why you didn't come.
# Alphabet Romanization: Ta bu lai de yuanyin wo zhidao, dan ni de yuanyin wo bu zhidao.
# Tang, Sze-Wing. "A parametric approach to NP ellipsis in Mandarin and Cantonese." Journal of East Asian Linguistics 20 (2011): 107-115.

说到方法，他修车的方法不怎么，修电脑的___还可以。
----
说到方法，他修车的方法不怎么，修电脑的方法还可以。
# Translation: Speaking of methods, his method of repairing cars is not very good, but his method of repairing computers is okay.
# Alphabet Romanization: Shuo dao fangfa, ta xiuche de fangfa bu zenme, xiu diannao de fangfa hai keyi.
# Tang, Sze-Wing. "A parametric approach to NP ellipsis in Mandarin and Cantonese." Journal of East Asian Linguistics 20 (2011): 107-115.

说到原因，他没来的原因合理，但做错事的___不合理。
----
说到原因，他没来的原因合理，但做错事的原因不合理。
# Translation: Speaking of reasons, his reason for not coming is reasonable, but his reason for doing something wrong is unreasonable.
# Alphabet Romanization: Shuo dao yuanyin, ta mei lai de yuanyin heli, dan zuo cuoshi de yuanyin buheli.
# Tang, Sze-Wing. "A parametric approach to NP ellipsis in Mandarin and Cantonese." Journal of East Asian Linguistics 20 (2011): 107-115.

张三喜欢李四，但是玛丽不喜欢___
----
张三喜欢李四，但是玛丽不喜欢李四。
# Translation: Zhang San likes Li Si, but Mary doesn't like Li Si.
# Alphabet Romanization: Zhang San xihuan Li Si, danshi Mary bu xihuan Li Si.
# Cheng, Hsu-Te Johnny. "Argument ellipsis in Mandarin Chinese." Proceedings of the 23rd North American Conference on Chinese Linguistics (NACCL-23). Vol. 2. 2011.

张三喜欢李四，但是___不喜欢玛丽。
----
张三喜欢李四，但是李四不喜欢玛丽。
# Translation: Zhang San likes Li Si, but Li Si doesn't like Mary.
# Alphabet Romanization: Zhang San xihuan Li Si, danshi Li Si bu xihuan Mary.
# Cheng, Hsu-Te Johnny. "Argument ellipsis in Mandarin Chinese." Proceedings of the 23rd North American Conference on Chinese Linguistics (NACCL-23). Vol. 2. 2011.

张三说___喜欢李四。
----
张三说张三喜欢李四。
# Translation: Zhang San said that Zhang San likes Li Si.
# Alphabet Romanization: Zhang San shuo Zhang San xihuan Li Si.
# Cheng, Hsu-Te Johnny. "Argument ellipsis in Mandarin Chinese." Proceedings of the 23rd North American Conference on Chinese Linguistics (NACCL-23). Vol. 2. 2011.
